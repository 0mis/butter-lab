What is being solved

Matter first, pixels second.

BL–05 evolves shared-face butter thickness and eight vertical enthalpy layers on the GPU. Heat arrives through implicit contact, forced convection, long-wave radiation, and partitioned solar absorption. A literature-informed solid-fat prior controls the crystal network, yield stress, and viscosity; the puddle moves through a conservative, positivity-preserving shallow flux.
